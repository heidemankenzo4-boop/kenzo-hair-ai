# Kenzo Hair AI

Your personalized hair growth & styling advisor. Enter your hair type (1A–4C) or describe your hair in your own words, and Kenzo Hair AI will generate a tailored plan including:

- **Personalized Hair Growth Plan** — step-by-step routines specific to your hair type
- **Best Hairstyles** — curated hairstyle recommendations that work with your natural texture
- **Product Recommendations** — featuring Based Bodyworks products matched to your hair's needs

## Hair Types Supported

| Type | Category | Subtypes |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Straight | 1A (fine & flat), 1B (medium body), 1C (coarse & thick) |
| 2 | Wavy | 2A (loose waves), 2B (defined S-waves), 2C (deep waves) |
| 3 | Curly | 3A (loose curls), 3B (springy ringlets), 3C (tight corkscrews) |
| 4 | Coily | 4A (defined coils), 4B (Z-pattern coils), 4C (tight zig-zag) |

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Select your hair type from the grid, or describe your hair in the text box
3. Receive your personalized AI-generated hair plan

## Tech Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no dependencies, no build step required. Just open and go.
